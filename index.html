Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in your web design packages?

Our comprehensive packages include custom responsive design, unlimited revisions, SEO optimization, free hosting for life, SSL certification, and 24/7 support. Each website is built with modern frameworks ensuring fast load times and mobile optimization.

How long does the design process take?

Most projects are completed within 2-4 weeks, depending on complexity. Our streamlined process includes discovery (3-5 days), design (5-7 days), development (7-14 days), and testing/launch (3-5 days). Rush services are available for time-sensitive projects.

What ongoing support do you provide?

We offer comprehensive maintenance packages including weekly backups, security updates, performance monitoring, content updates, and technical support. Our dedicated support team is available 24/7 via phone, email, or chat.

How do you handle website security?

Security is our top priority. We implement SSL certificates, regular security audits, malware scanning, and DDoS protection. Our hosting infrastructure includes daily backups and 24/7 monitoring to ensure your site remains secure and operational.

What are your payment terms?

We offer flexible payment plans starting at $199/month. Standard terms are 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining balance due upon project completion. We accept all major credit cards, ACH transfers, and PayPal.
